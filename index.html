About Us

We invite you to join us as we worship and fellowship in God's Word and enjoy the liberty that His Spirit brings every Wednesdays, Fridays, and Sundays. Remember you are blessed and highly favored.

We praise God for His faithfulness to us as a ministry since our inception 19 years ago. We brace ourselves for unprecedented growth as Bethel seeks to faithfully carry out the mandate of our Lord, to reach the nations with the life-transforming Gospel of Jesus Christ and "Win The Lost At All Costs.

Rev. Oliver T. Russ
