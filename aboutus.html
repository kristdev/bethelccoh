Welcome to the Bethel World Outreach Church - HISTORY OF BETHEL CITY CENTER OF HOPE -

While serving as Pastors of Bethel Central (Bethel City Center Of Life) for three and a half years, the Lord spoke to the heart of missionary couple Oliver and Ella Russ to take God and the church to the people of Douala City Center.

In June of 2004 the Russes started a cell group in Bali with 15 persons. Later, on February 6, 2005 at “Maison de combatant” in Bonanjo the Bali cell group became Bethel City Center of Hope.
Two years later, we sent out our first missionaries to Bertoua in the east of Cameroon. Three years later, on June 1, 2008 disaster hit and we lost everything in a raging fire that demonstrated no mercy. In one night, we were back at ZERO and poised to begin again. How have we made it? “It’s only God!”
In spite of the many storms we have come through, the church continues to grow.

In 2012 we purchased a parcel of land in New Bell – Bonadiwoto near the Douala international airport where there is an ongoing project to build a one thousand seats church.
